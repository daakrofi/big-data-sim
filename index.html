Strategic Executive Mandate

Welcome, Strategy Task Force. Highland Studios is preparing to commit its resources to its next major development project. Game production is a multi-million-pound, long-term commitment taking 3 to 5 years. Instinct is not enough; you must leverage analytical evidence to justify the studio's project path.

Our Capability & History

Highland Studios is historically recognized for two core strengths:

  • Racing Simulation: Excellent track record, reliable sales performance, stable reviews, and highly competent internal physics engine development.
  • Skateboarding: Recent critical and commercial success with Streetline Skate, showing exceptional ability in expressive motion, physics-driven styling, and community advocacy.
  • Early Studio History: At the start of the studio's existence, Highland launched LifeSpace, a life simulation title released 15 years ago. The project remains part of the studio's archive and provides historical context for any discussion of returning to that category.

Task Force Goal

Your task force must inspect available data along the customer journey, analyze internal studio readiness, assess the competitive environment, and submit a final recommendation report.

Use the portal to decide which evidence matters, which evidence is limited or biased, and how the available data should shape Highland Studios' next project commitment.

Deliverable: A clear project recommendation supported by specific analytics evidence and explicit discussion of data limitations.

Project Options to Evaluate

Option 1: Racing Sequel

Create a follow-up to our highly rated motorsport titles. Utilizes proven physics systems and appeals to a reliable, built-in audience.

Option 2: Skateboarding Sequel

A direct sequel to Streetline Skate, capitalizing on community excitement, expressive customization, and skateboarding culture.

Option 3: Open-World Street Sports Game

Expand skateboarding physics to parkour, biking, and street culture. Focuses on social multiplayer spaces and streaming popularity.

Option 4: Life Simulation Reboot

A complete reimagining of the life simulation genre. Massive commercial potential, with an opportunity to build the studio's capacity in a different genre.

Option 6: Motorsport Management & Lifestyle

A tactical management game merging racing metrics with lifestyle elements, driver relationships, and custom team sponsorships.
